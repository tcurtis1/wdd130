History

Founded in 1998, Moab Whitewater Adventures has been at the forefront of offering thrilling and safe white water rafting experiences in the heart of Utah. Nestled in the scenic landscapes of Moab, our company is dedicated to providing unforgettable adventures for thrill-seekers and nature enthusiasts alike. With over two decades of experience, our team of expert guides ensures that every trip down the mighty Colorado River is not only exhilarating but also educational, highlighting the rich geological and cultural history of the region.

At Moab Whitewater Adventures, our mission is to deliver top-tier outdoor experiences that foster a deep connection with nature. We believe that rafting is more than just a sport; it's an opportunity to immerse oneself in the breathtaking beauty of Utah's canyons and rivers. Our commitment to safety, environmental stewardship, and customer satisfaction drives us to continuously improve our services and preserve the pristine environment in which we operate. Whether you're a seasoned rafter or a first-time adventurer, our goal is to provide a journey that you will cherish for a lifetime.

Moab Whitewater Adventures offers a range of trips tailored to suit all levels of experience and excitement. From half-day excursions to multi-day expeditions, our trips cover some of the most iconic sections of the Colorado River, including the famed Cataract Canyon. Each journey is led by our highly trained guides who are not only skilled in navigating the rapids but also passionate about sharing their knowledge of the local flora, fauna, and history. As you paddle through towering red rock canyons and tackle thrilling rapids, you'll gain a deeper appreciation for the natural world and the unique beauty of Moab.

We are deeply committed to protecting the environment and promoting sustainable tourism practices. Moab Whitewater Adventures implements eco-friendly measures across all operations, from minimizing waste and using biodegradable products to supporting local conservation efforts. We work closely with environmental organizations to ensure that our activities have minimal impact on the delicate ecosystems of the Colorado River. By choosing Moab Whitewater Adventures, you are supporting a company that values and actively contributes to the preservation of Utah's natural heritage.
